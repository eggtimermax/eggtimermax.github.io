Frequently Asked Questions

How does EggTimer Max calculate cooking time?

EggTimer Max uses the Williams formula — a physics-based model that takes into account the weight of the egg, its starting temperature, and the altitude at which you're cooking (which affects the boiling point of water). The result is the exact time needed to reach your preferred doneness.

What does "Pro" unlock?

Pro features include the live boiling-water animation, altitude-aware boiling point correction, a live Activity, and the ability to cook multiple eggs at once with individual doneness settings. Pro is unlocked via a one-time tip of €2.99 or more.

I bought Pro but it's not showing up

Go to Settings → Restore purchases. If the issue persists, contact us at broelando@icloud.com.

Does the app work without an internet connection?

Yes — EggTimer Max works completely offline. No account required.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

Overview

EggTimer Max is designed with privacy in mind. The app does not require an account and does not collect or transmit personal data.

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal information. All your settings (egg weight, temperature preference, doneness) are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults and are never sent to any server.

Purchases

In-app purchases and tips are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Analytics

EggTimer Max does not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

Third-party services

No third-party SDKs that collect data are included in EggTimer Max. Future versions may include Apple's own frameworks (such as StoreKit) which are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

EggTimer Max does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
