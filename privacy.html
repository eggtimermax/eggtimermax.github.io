Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2025

Overview

EggTimer Max is built with privacy in mind. The app does not require an account and does not collect, store or transmit any personal data.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect any personal information. Your settings — egg weight, starting temperature and preferred doneness — are saved locally on your device and never leave it.

Purchases

In-app purchases and tips are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment details.

Analytics & tracking

EggTimer Max contains no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not track how you use the app, and there are no advertising identifiers.

Third-party services

No data-collecting third-party SDKs are included. The app uses Apple's own frameworks (such as StoreKit for purchases), which are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

EggTimer Max does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Reach us via the contact page.
